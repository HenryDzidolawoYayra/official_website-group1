Academic Journey

Miss Linda Otoo is a trailblazing academic, researcher, and advocate for technological advancement in education. With over a decade of experience in the field of Information Technology, she has established herself as a leading figure at the University of Cape Coast (UCC), Ghana.

Born and raised in Ghana, Miss Otoo developed an early interest in computers and technology. She pursued her undergraduate studies in Computer Science at the University of Cape Coast, where she graduated with top honors. Her academic excellence earned her a scholarship to pursue a Master's degree in Information Technology, specializing in web development and database systems.
